## 📌 Executive Summary
A healthcare analytics dashboard designed to track operational KPIs, patient trends, and reporting metrics, enabling stakeholders to monitor performance and improve healthcare delivery.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Healthcare organizations require real-time visibility into:
- Patient trends
- Operational efficiency
- Quality metrics

---

## 📊 Dataset
- Patient records
- Visit data
- Treatment metrics
- Operational KPIs

---

## 📈 Key Metrics
- Patient volume
- Visit trends
- Treatment distribution
- Operational efficiency KPIs

---

## 🔍 Analysis
- Trend analysis across time
- Department-level performance
- Patient segmentation

---

## 💡 Insights
- Peak patient periods identified
- Variability across departments
- Opportunities for resource optimization

---

## 📊 Dashboard Features
- KPI monitoring dashboard
- Time-series analysis
- Department filtering
- Interactive visualizations

---

## 👤 Stakeholder Use Cases
- Operations teams → monitor performance
- Executives → track KPIs
- Analysts → explore trends
